Stories on Gender Equality

In Kenya, a recent initiative launched by grassroots organizations is empowering rural women through access to microloans and agricultural training. Many of these women, once economically dependent, are now leading their communities in sustainable farming. The project is not only enhancing livelihoods but also challenging traditional gender roles in rural society.

Iceland continues to lead in gender equality by enforcing pay transparency laws that require companies to prove equal pay for equal work. This policy has significantly reduced the wage gap and serves as a global example. Women in Iceland now enjoy one of the highest levels of economic and political participation in the world.
